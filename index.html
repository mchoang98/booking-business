📌 Project Plan – Hotel Room Overflow/Allocation App (Phase 1)

     

Professional summary of the room allocation platform development.

   
 
   
       
     

1. Project Overview

     

        We will develop a **mobile allocation app** designed to handle **customer overflow** from hotels, redirecting or booking them into available rooms at nearby, collaborating hotel partners.         This application serves as a B2B tool to manage surplus customers efficiently. The platform is designed for future expansion into other industries with high demand/supply fluctuation.      

   
       
     

2. Main User Types

     
       
         

👤 Customers (End-Users)

         
               
  • **Note:** Not directly available to customers in Phase 1 (B2B staff tool).
    •            
  • Interaction is managed entirely by hotel staff.
    •          
       
       
         

🏢 Business Owners (Hotels)

         
               
  • Submit overflow requests (Requesting Hotel).
    •            
  • Receive, accept, reject, or propose new assignment solutions (Partner Hotel).
    •            
  • Manage room reallocation assignments in real-time.
    •

4. Allocation Flow Diagram

     
           
  1. Request Submission: Hotel with customer overflow submits a room request via the app (specifying details).
    2.        
  2. Alert Distribution: Notifications are broadcast to nearby, collaborating hotel partners.
    3.        
  3. First-to-Accept Allocation (F.C.F.S): The first partner hotel in the area with available rooms that accepts the request secures the allocation.
    4.        
  4. Passive Rejection: Hotels that are unable to accept (due to lack of speed or lack of availability) passively let the request expire or be claimed. No manual rejection is required.
    5.        
  5. Real-time Update: Both the requesting hotel and the receiving partner hotel receive real-time confirmation of the allocation.
    6.
