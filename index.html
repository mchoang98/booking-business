Booking App for Hotel Customer Overflow

1. Project Overview

Develop a B2B room reservation system connecting hotels with a surplus of customers (Host Hotel) to nearby hotels (Partner Hotels) with room availability. The system focuses on efficient, real-time customer re-allocation and is designed for future expansion into other corporate booking solutions.

2. Main User Types

  • Business Owners (Host/Partner Hotels): Receive overflow alerts; accept, manage, or propose new appointments in real-time.
  • Customers: Not available to customers (yet).
